# SEEKR Manufacturing Readiness

Created: 2026-05-04

## V1 Position

Use integrated development aircraft when hardware begins. Do not optimize custom manufacturing before the software/replay/safety platform proves value.

## V2 Readiness Items

- Mass/power/endurance BOM.
- Supplier list and alternates.
- Assembly QA checklist.
- Vibration and thermal tests.
- Prop guard durability tests.
- Payload calibration procedure.
- Flight-controller parameter baseline.
- Maintenance and spare-parts plan.

## Pushback

A professional SAR product is not a hobby BOM. Supportability, calibration, documentation, and safety evidence matter as much as raw part cost.
