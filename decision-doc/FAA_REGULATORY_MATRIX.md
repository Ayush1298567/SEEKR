## Regulatory Rules For Messaging

- Do not claim routine BVLOS availability without dated legal verification.
- Do not claim autonomous SAR deployment readiness from simulator evidence.
- Distinguish simulator, controlled training, public-safety COA, waiver, and future Part 108 operations.
