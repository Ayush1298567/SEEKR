# SEEKR GTM And Pricing

Created: 2026-05-04

## Target Customer

Start with SAR, fire, US&R training teams, and public-safety design partners who can run controlled training pilots. Do not begin with broad FEMA headquarters procurement.

## Offer Shape

- Pilot package: simulator/GCS, training scenario support, evidence export, operator feedback sessions.
- Hardware pilot later: one integrated VIO aircraft, then three-drone controlled demo.
- Product package: aircraft, GCS software, training, support, maintenance, and compliance documentation.

## Pricing Direction

Use bundled pricing. Hardware-only pricing misses integration, training, support, evidence workflow, and safety-case cost.

## Differentiation

- GPS-denied SAR workflow.
- Deterministic replay/evidence package.
- AI advisory boundary with validator-owned authority.
- Map-centric coverage and detection workflow.
- Safety/regulatory documentation from the start.
